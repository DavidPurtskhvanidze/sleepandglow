blanket

The Sleep&Glow weighted blanket consists of 11 layers. This is a unique design that ensures a perfectly even distribution of the weighting agent, glass microbeads, inside the blanket. Due to this, you get the maximum effects from the deep sensory pressure therapy, since the weight is distributed evenly throughout your body. You quickly plunge into a state of relaxation and sleep deeper all night long.

The uniform
pressure allows you to:

Relax and fall asleep faster
Not wake up and toss and turn at night
Experience the enveloping effect of a hug
