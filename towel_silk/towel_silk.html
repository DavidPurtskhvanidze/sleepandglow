ADVANCED SILK TOWELS

Engineered for Skin’s Most Vulnerable Moments

Care When Skin Needs It Most

Right after cleansing, your skin is freshly exposed — pores open, natural oils reduced, and the protective barrier temporarily weakened. It’s the moment when ordinary towels can do the most damage — drying, tugging, and introducing bacteria.
Our advanced silk towels are designed for this critical moment. Infused with skincare actives and made from the softest silk, they protect, calm, and enhance your skin when it’s most in need.
